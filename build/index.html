Platform for remote control transport and robots

The system consists of a module for controlling engines and mechanisms of a remote device and transmitting a video signal and telemetry, as well as an online platform for managing the device

Platform scheme

Our real-time FPV platform helps to send the managing commands and receive video from remote device with no delays (<200 ms)

  • Web platform for remote (long distance) control of robots from home, office or other places with the Internet
  • Control is carried out using a conventional Sony Playstation joystick (or equivalent) and a PC (or tablet)
  • The control is performed from the first person (FPV) using a camera mounted on the robot
  • The operator can control the movement of the robot and perform other available actions using the function keys on the joystick

Platform benefits

Mecht.ai help you to increase efficiency of your company services and reduce costs and risks

  • An hour of work of a remotely controlled robot costs 1.7 times cheaper than performing similar functions by a person
  • In a unit of time, the operator of a remote robot can perform 1.2 more tasks than when performing them physically.
  • The “endless order” technology, similar to that in taxi services, reduces downtime
  • Process transparency enables continuous process improvement and risk reduction
